The Things Gateway

The Things Gateway is a gateway which is easy to use and install, is low cost and will allow you to contribute to the network from your home. The range is around 4 miles and perfect for citizens to create their smart city.

All designs and code will be open sourced so anybody will be able to make its own gateway.

We will launch the campaign early October. Find below all the products that are going to be included

The Things Gateway

The first open source LoRa WAN gateway. Cover an area up to 10km straight from your home.
